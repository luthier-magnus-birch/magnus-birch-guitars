I'm Magnus Birch

I am a 24-year-old Danish luthier currently residing in Belgium, where I have been passionately crafting guitars for the past four years. My journey into the world of lutherie began with a deep appreciation for music and woodworking, which naturally led me to the art of guitar-making. I am honing my skills and expanding my knowledge at the Internation Lutherie School of Antwerp, ILSA, a renowned school for luthiers. My work is characterized by a meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to creating instruments that not only look beautiful but also deliver exceptional sound quality. Every guitar I build is a testament to my dedication to this craft, blending traditional techniques with modern innovations to meet the unique needs of each musician.
